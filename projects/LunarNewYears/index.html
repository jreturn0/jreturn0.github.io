Description

Interactive installation using TouchDesigner, themed around the year of the dragon and snake respectively. Made for Cadillac to exhibit at a sponsored Lunar New Year event, commisioned by Pixel Pusher. The user could directly control the particles using their hands via tracking from a leap motion installed on a podium in front of the screen. Allowing the image to come apart and have the particles follow their hands, forming back to the original image when moving their hands away from the podium.
