About me

I'm a programmer, game dev, and artist from Ontario. I started programming in highschool and have loved it ever since. Since then I've worked on art installations, games, and research projects. I Studied Game Programming at Humber College where I learned C++, graphics (mainly OpenGL and Vulkan), and many low-level concepts. I enjoy working lower level as I've built 2 game engines before but love being able to be creative in my work espcially in higher level areas like the installations I've done
